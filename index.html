About Me

I am 44 years old, I was born in raised in Eugene, Oregon (GO DUCKS). I am a Field Service Engineer for Hyundai Motor America, and have been for the past four years. After serving in the United States Navy in the 90s, I relocated here to southern California. I started in the automotive field here at the Riverside Auto Center in 2000. I have worked for Ponitac, Buick, Suzuki, Saturn, and finally Hyundai in 2006 as an automotive technician. In 2013 I won the "World's Best Hyundai Technician" through a compition held in Seoul Korea with 100 competitors from 52 countries. In 2015 I accepted my current position to challege myself in a new roll in my field, and to open new doors. I currently "code" as a hobby and I am slated to assist / back-up the current lead in my office as the internial web developer.

I live here in Riverside, I am married with 4 kids, two Grandkids, and two dogs (a Shephard and a Lab). I love live music, and all car related activities (races, shows, etc.). Fun fact, I have seen most rock / metal bands there are, I believe I have an addiction.
